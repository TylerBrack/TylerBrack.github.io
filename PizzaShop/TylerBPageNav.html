History

We are a family owned business in operation since 1952. Our Uncle Dino started the family business in 1952 and 3 generations have now worked in the pizzeria.

We have been located in the same building in Milwaukee's trendy Brady Street neighborhood since inception. Pizza is our passion and we hope to continue to share it with Milwaukee for years to come.
