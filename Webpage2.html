The Bird: A article about trap shooting

The Interview

The man the myth the legend Evan Platz, one of the best all-around shooters on the team. Here I sat down with Evan and asked him some questions Who had the greatest impact on your shooting career?
  • Who had the greatest impact on your shooting career? “I can’t narrow it down to one specific person, but a combination of my teammates and coaches advice”. Said Evan.
  • What ammo do you use? “I use mostly prefer Winchester universals but will shoot whatever was on sale that week at Gander mountain”. Said Evan.
  • Do you handle the pressures of competition? “I would have to say I do fairly well dealing with the fierce competition on the trap range”. Said Evan.
